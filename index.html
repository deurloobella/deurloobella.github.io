Build the perfect mukbang spread

  1. Anchor: choose the “main” (ramen, chicken, seafood boil).
  2. Textures: add crunch + chew (pickles, fries, tteokbokki).
  3. Sauce flight: 2–3 dips (garlic, spicy, sweet).
  4. Reset: something fresh (cucumber, fruit, slaw).

ASMR-friendly picks

  • Crispy chicken / karaage
  • Pickles + crunchy veggies
  • Chips + salsa / guac
  • Sticky rice cakes (tteokbokki)
  • Ice desserts (bingsu, shaved ice)
